TCC Cyber Club History and Awards:

  • 05-03-2024

    TCC Cyber Conference

    Past and current leadership come together to pratice being guest speakers! The speakers were Nicholas Wiehr, Raymond Aiello, Ethan Phelps, and Sebastian Tubbs.

    The TCC Cyber Club once again took first place in the Community College division! This year the team was Nicholas Wiehr, Raymond Aiello, Ethan Phelps, Sebastian Tubbs, Dahvian Brandon, and Matt West.

    CVCSA hosted CyberForge 2023 at Tidewater Community College with help from the TCC Cyber Club. Many TCC and non TCC students took part filling the auditorium.

    Within the Experienced Student Bracket TC4 got 93rd of 454. We had a team of 4 compossed of Nicholas Wiehr, Raymond Aiello, Ethan Phelps, and Colson Faustin.

    In the standard Bracket TC4 got 227th of 3593. For Spring 2023 we had Nicholas Wiehr, Keith (Jacob) Betts, and Colson Faustin as a 3 man team.

    CVCSA hosted CyberForge 2023 at Christopher Newport University. There TC4 had Keith (Jacob) Betts, Jonathon Chase D'Amore, Colson Faustin, and others take part.
